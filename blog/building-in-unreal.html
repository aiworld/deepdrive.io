Rebuilding Deepdrive on Unreal Engine

TLDR; Deepdrive has been rebuilt without GTA and now includes support for multiple cameras, depth, and running natively on Linux - All open source on Unreal Engine. Check it out!

Background - or skip to Unreal stuff

A little over a year ago, I worked with the OpenAI team to release an integration for GTAV into the Universe platform. The goal was to make one of the most complex video games available to researchers to experiment with methods like deep reinforcement learning for self-driving.

However, it turned out that TakeTwo was not as receptive to people using GTAV

I do want to say that I apologize for not having gotten TakeTwo's blessing to use GTA. I know that many of you had spent time with Deepdrive 1.0 and Deepdrive Universe, and am deeply sorry for not being able to continue to support your efforts. I hope that rebuilding the project on Unreal Engine will be able to restore things you found valuable in the GTA version, and eventually do much more.

Before Deepdrive Universe was launched, I had started work at Uber's Advanced Technologies Group on their perception team. It was an opportunity to apply deep learning in real cars as well as incorporate the concerns of real self-driving back into Deepdrive.

I found that there was surprising overlap between real and virtual training. Things like reducing sampling error when train and test distributions are not IID.
